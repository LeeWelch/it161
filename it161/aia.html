Type of website Marketing
Client's goals To boost online visibility, increase income, and streamline booking via a booking form.
Audience Issues Approach
Business Travelers: Their age group is 30-65, all genders; the average income for business travelers is $82,000 per year; their job varies but involves frequent travel; they are college-educated. They’re skilled with computers and familiar with website navigation. They use Chrome, Firefox, possibly Microsoft Edge, or other for older businesspeople. They need fast and reliable internet for work, and they have a high-resolution monitor. They’re often using a laptop to navigate websites.
  • They need a pet sitter they can consistently trust and rely on for their business trips.
  • They need a sitter that offers multi-day boarding services.
  • They need a sitter with availability to match their travel schedule.
  • They often need to book last minute, they need a quick response when contacting the sitter.
  • Publish certificates, qualifications, and testimonials from existing customers.
  • Clearly publish all services and rates.
  • Clearly publish availability - create an online calendar that is updated frequently.
  • Make contact information easy to find, create an online booking system for instant booking.
Holiday Customers: Their age group is 20+, all genders, they have jobs that allow them to take off for the holidays, and they have all education levels. Violette Morganti only offers services within a 1-mile radius of downtown Seattle, so their clients generally reside in central Seattle. The income for this customer group reflects the average Seattle income of $52,000 per year for individuals. They have good internet access, like most people in downtown Seattle, and they may use any browser or platform. They are likely to have moderate to excellent computer skills and experience.
  • These customers may not book pet sitters outside of the holidays, so they’re unfamiliar with the process and may be nervous and/or overwhelmed.
  • They’re anxious about leaving their pet with a pet sitter, and need to find someone they can trust. They want good communication with frequent check ins and updates during their pet’s stay.
  • They need a sitter that offers multi day services.
  • They want to know if there are any rate or schedule changes for holiday bookings.
  • Simple and easy to navigate website with an excellent user experience that conveys professionalism.
  • Publish certificates, qualifications and testimonials from existing customers - ensure that the testimonials chosen touch on responsiveness and communication.
  • Clearly publish services.
  • Clearly post holiday rates and availability, create an online calendar.
Senior Customers: Their age group is 65+; they are usually retired and don’t work, but they may work part-time or volunteer. This group can be any gender; their income comes from their retirement fund; and they have all levels of education. Their computer skills and abilities will usually be low to moderate; they’ll use the platform they have the most experience with, often Windows. They’ll use the default browser on their device, often Microsoft Edge, and they don’t need high-speed internet for work, so they may just have sufficient speed for streaming services and social media. They favor tablets, desktops, and their phones over laptops and may not have high-resolution monitors on their devices.
  • They need help providing enough exercise for their pets. They’re likely home so they won’t usually need boarding services, they want information about drop ins, walks, park outings.
  • They may have low computer comfort and skill levels, they need an easy to navigate experience.
  • 25% of seniors have visual impairments, they need an accessible website that works well with assistive technologies.
  • Clearly publish all services and rates with descriptions of services, what each service includes, what clients can expect.
  • Website should have an excellent user experience and be easy to navigate for all computer skill/comfort levels.
  • Create an accessible website that’s compatible with assistive technologies.
