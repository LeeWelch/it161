About Me

Hi, I’m Lee Welch, and this is my portal. All of my projects so far are linked above; feel free to click around to see what I’ve been working on.

I’m a semi-recent transplant to Seattle, and I’m currently delving into the world of web development as a student at Seattle Central College. Through the wide array of topics in my first three courses—Web Authoring 1 (IT161), Software Development Tools (IT115), and Business Productivity Apps (BTM 111), I’m beginning my journey to become a web developer.

My studies so far have ranged from mastering all things Microsoft Office to learning the basics of HMTL, CSS, and online collaboration tools. This includes exploring ways to utilize GitHub and using a command line to spin up servers and LAMP stacks in Google Cloud. I’ve worked on multiple group projects thus far, branching and merging code with other students and collaborating on a website hosted on GitHub.

By the end of this quarter, I’ll have built a customized website to meet my client’s needs and increase their online reach. I’m excited to help a local business build their customer base and flourish online.
